Our Mission

Reducing health disparities through action

We provide free public health education, hands-on clinical skills workshops, healthcare career mentorship, and preventive health screenings to underserved populations throughout Los Angeles County.

01

Health Education

Interactive workshops on chronic disease prevention, nutrition, mental health, and health literacy for all ages.

02

Mentorship & Skills Training

Mentoring high school and college students about healthcare careers, plus hands-on suturing and vital signs workshops.

03

Preventive Screenings

Free blood pressure, glucose, BMI, and vision screenings to identify health risks early and connect people with care.

What We Do

Our Programs & Initiatives

From hands-on clinical workshops to community screenings, we are building a healthier Los Angeles.

🏥

Mobile Health Screenings

Free preventive health screenings in underserved neighborhoods — blood pressure, glucose, BMI, and vision assessments at no cost.

🩺

Suturing & Vital Signs Workshops

Hands-on clinical skills training where students learn suturing techniques, how to take vital signs, and fundamental patient care skills.

🎓

Healthcare Career Mentorship

Mentoring high school and college students exploring careers in medicine, nursing, public health, and allied health professions.

🧠

Mental Health Initiatives

Destigmatizing mental health through open forums, stress management workshops, and connecting individuals with support services.

🌍

Community Partnerships

Collaborating with LA shelters, schools, faith-based organizations, and community centers to embed health resources locally.

📋

Health Literacy Workshops

Educational sessions on chronic disease prevention, nutrition fundamentals, and navigating the healthcare system for underserved communities.
